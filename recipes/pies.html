Pies

Nothing beats a good pie. Whether sweet or savory, pie is always a treat.

The secret to a good pie is in the crust. The ingredients are simple enough, but making sure that you get the proper lamination for a flaky crust can be tricky.

All pies start with two simple ingredients for your crust.

Ingredients

  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Goat Butter

From there you can expirement with different fillings. I've provided you a few variations here.

All pies are cooked the same; following the simple step below.

Instructions

  1. Select ingredients from your pack.
  2. Combine ingredients in cooking pot.


Fruit Pie

Fruit pies are can be made by combining any fruit with sugar for a sweet treat.

The fruit that you use will determine the effects of the pie. Always take things like the weather and local monsters into account when selecting your ingredients for fruit pie.

There are two special fruit pies that we'll discus seperately.

Ingredients

  • Crust Ingredients
  • Cane Sugar
  • One of the following:
    • Fleet-Lotus Seeds
    • Hearty Durian
    • Hydromelon
    • Mighty Bananas
    • Palm Fruit
    • Spicy Pepper
    • Volt Fruit
    • Wildberry


Apple Pie

Apples are a delicious staple of hylian cuisine. They are in all sorts of recipes. Personally I think they're best when made into a pie.

Apple pies have a special place separate from the various other fruit pies. They are made the same as any fruit pie.

Ingredients

  • Crust Ingredients
  • Cane Sugar
  • Apple


Pumpkin Pie

The Fortified Pumpkin, A major trade crop out of Kakariko Village. These pumpkins are used in several wonderful recipes; one of which is pumpkin pie.

Pumpkin pie is just as simple as any other pie. Eating it will make you feel like you can take on the world.

Ingredients

  • Crust Ingredients
  • Cane Sugar
  • Fortified Pumpkin


Meat Pie

Meat pies are savory and delicous. They are often best when you need a hearty meal to restore your vitality.

Fillings for a meat pie are simple. A dash of rock salt and your favorite meat.

Ingredients

  • Crust Ingredients
  • Rock Salt
  • One of the following:
    • Raw Bird Thigh
    • Raw Bird Drumstick
    • Raw Gourmet Meat
    • Raw Meat
    • Raw Prime Meat
    • Raw Whole Bird


Fish Pie

Fish are a staple for many Hylian's diet. There are 20 species of fish that live in Hyrule and all can be made into delicious pies.

With that much variety you can eat Fish Pie every day and not get bored.

Fish pies are made just like meat pies. Add some salt to your seafood selection and cook away.
