Pies

Nothing beats a good pie. Whether sweet or savory, pie is always a treat.

The secret to a good pie is in the crust. The ingredients are simple enough, but making sure that you get the proper lamination for a flaky crust can be tricky.

All pies start with two simple ingredients for your crust.

Ingredients

  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Goat Butter

From there you can expirement with different fillings. I've provided you a few variations here.

All pies are cooked the same; following the simple step below.

Instructions

  1. Select ingredients from your pack.
  2. Combine ingredients in cooking pot.


Fruit Pie

Fruit pies are can be made by combining any fruit with sugar for a sweet treat. The fruit that you use will determine the effects of the pie. Always take things like the weather and local monsters into account when selecting your ingredients for fruit pie.

Ingredients

  • Crust Ingredients
  • Cane Sugar
  • One of the following:
    • Fleet-Lotus Seeds
    • Hearty Durian
    • Hydromelon
    • Mighty Bananas
    • Palm Fruit
    • Spicy Pepper
    • Volt Fruit
    • Wildberry

Meat Pie

Meat pies are savory and delicous. They are often best when you need a hearty meal to restore your vitality.

Fillings for a meat pie are simple. A dash of rock salt and your favorite meat.

Ingredients

  • Crust Ingredients
  • Rock Salt
  • One of the following:
    • Raw Bird Thigh
    • Raw Bird Drumstick
    • Raw Gourmet Meat
    • Raw Meat
    • Raw Prime Meat
    • Raw Whole Bird
