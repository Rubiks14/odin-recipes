Traveller's Guide to Stews

There is nothing that warms up the insides like a good stew. A stew can refill your vitality and get you set for a long journey or a chilly night.

Stew is a common cuisine in Hyrule. You can make it out of most anything so it's an easy meal to make for your family.

All stews have the same common ingredients that form the base

Ingredients

  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Goat Butter
  • Fresh Milk

Once you know the base you can try to make some different options. Below are some examples of stews that you will commonly see.

All stews shown on this page are simple to cook and follow the same instructions.

Instructions

  1. Select ingredients from your pack.
  2. Combine ingredients in cooking pot.


Meat Stews

There is nothing that can fill your belly and warm you up quite like a good meat stew.

The kind of stew you end up with depends on the type of meat used.

Ingredients

  • Stew Base Ingredients
  • One of the Following:
    • Raw Meat
    • Raw Prime Meat
    • Raw Gourmet Meat


Carrot Stew

There are two types of carrots found in Hyrule: Swift Carrots and Endura Carrots. Both make excellent stews.

Swift carrots are grown in the towns so they are pretty abundant. Most carrot stew that you'll find is made from these

Endura carrots have to be harvested in the wild. Typically they are foraged around Satori Mountain.

Ingredients

  • Stew Base Ingredients
  • One of the following:
    • Swift Carrot
    • Endura Carrot


Pumpkin Stew

Fortified Pumpkins are so versatile. If you've looked at the pies you've already seen them there.

Pumpkin stew is another great use for the Fortified Pumpkin. When cooked down these pumpkins turn incredibly soft and are easily pureed into a silky smooth stew.

Ingredients

  • Stew Base Ingredients
  • Fortified Pumpkin
